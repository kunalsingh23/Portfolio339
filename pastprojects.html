Past Projects

Cubs Business Analytics

After my sophomore year at Michigan, I was a business analytics intern for Wrigley Field in Chicago. I created a model in Python to forecast sales and purchasing habits of consumers at Wrigley field on a game-by-game basis.
The model found the 5-most similar past events using Euclidean distance and generated roughly 5 Million dollars in revenue. The model outputs optimal prices for foods and beverages at an event with given parameters.
